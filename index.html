Our Process

1

Consultation

Listen to your needs, business goals, and ideas and vision for the website. Determine the target audience and features needed.

2

PLanning & Analysis

Create a website structure (sitemap) and user flow. Develop a design plan and technology to be used.

3

Desain & Revision

Create a mockup or visual appearance of the website according to your needs. Revise the design to your liking.

4

Development

Build the website based on the approved design. Add necessary features, such as forms, payment integration, or others.

5

Testing

Checking website functionality, speed, and compatibility across various devices and browsers. Ensure there are no bugs or errors.

6

Launch

The website is ready to be launched and accessed by the public. Ensure all elements are functioning properly once live.

7

Maintenance & Support

Provide post-launch support, such as content updates, bug fixes, or adding new features. Provide regular maintenance services to maintain website performance.
